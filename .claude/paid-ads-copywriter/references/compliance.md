# Compliance Reference: Regulated Ad Categories

Use this file when writing copy for health, wellness, finance, or other regulated categories.
Always flag compliance concerns to the client — you are a copywriter, not a lawyer.

---

## Health & Wellness (Most DTC Supplement / CPG Clients)

### Google Ads Policy: Healthcare & Medicines
- Supplements must comply with local laws — Google restricts unapproved pharmaceuticals
- No misleading claims about efficacy
- "Clinically proven" requires substantiation — avoid unless the client has studies
- Weight loss: avoid before/after, guaranteed results, rapid loss language

### Meta Ads Policy: Health & Body Image
Meta is stricter than Google. Violations lead to ad rejection and account flags.

**Prohibited:**
- Before and after imagery
- Ideal body language ("perfect body", "slim down fast")
- Language implying personal health status ("your diabetes", "your anxiety")
- Shock/scare tactics about health
- Guaranteed outcomes
- Weight/BMI-specific claims

**Permitted (use these instead):**
- "Supports healthy digestion" ✅
- "Customers report feeling more energized" ✅
- "Formulated with [ingredient] to promote X" ✅
- "Designed for people who want [benefit]" ✅
- Third-person testimonials about experience (not clinical outcome)

### FTC Guidelines (US — applies to all copy)
- Testimonials must reflect typical results, or include a disclaimer: "Results not typical"
- Claims must be substantiated — if the client can't back it up, don't say it
- "Free" must actually be free — no hidden conditions
- Influencer/paid relationships must be disclosed in the ad

## Financial Products

### Google & Meta: Financial Services
- Income claims require disclaimers
- "Guaranteed returns" is prohibited on both platforms
- Risk disclosures often required in description copy
- Lending products: APR, terms, eligibility must be disclosed

**Safe language:**
- "Up to X% returns (results vary)" ✅
- "Some members earn..." ✅
- "No guaranteed outcomes" disclaimer ✅

---

## CBD / Cannabis

- **Google**: Does not allow CBD advertising (as of current policy). No workarounds.
- **Meta**: Restricted — CBD ads may be approved with restricted audiences and no health claims,
  but account-level approval is required. Flag to client immediately.
- Do not write copy for these categories without confirming the client has platform approval.

---

## Alcohol

- **Meta**: Restricted to 21+ targeting. No copy implying alcohol solves problems.
- **Google**: Permitted with age targeting. Follow local regulations.

---

## Copywriter's Compliance Checklist

Before delivering copy for a regulated category:

- [ ] Flagged the category to the client
- [ ] Replaced any guaranteed outcome language with "supports", "may", "designed to"
- [ ] Removed any before/after framing (Meta)
- [ ] Replaced body/weight language with feeling/energy language
- [ ] Added "results vary" qualifier to testimonial-based copy
- [ ] Noted which claims require substantiation from the client
- [ ] Recommended client legal review before launch

**Always include this note when delivering regulated copy:**

> ⚠️ This copy has been written to minimize policy risk, but ad platforms update policies
> frequently. Recommend client legal review before launch, especially for health claims.
> Final compliance responsibility rests with the advertiser.
